🍽️ ChopNow — Mood-Based Food Delivery App

ChopNow is a human-centered food delivery app that rethinks how people choose meals. Instead of forcing users to search endlessly, ChopNow adapts to human behavior — recommending food based on mood, budget, time, and location.

🚀 Concept

Most food apps assume users know what they want.

ChopNow challenges that.

It answers:

“I’m tired — what should I eat?”
“I only have 10k  what fits?”
“What can get here fastest?”

This is not just food delivery  it's decision support for real-life behavior.

🎯 Core Features
🧠 Mood-Based Recommendations

Users select how they feel:

Happy 😊
Tired 😩
Stressed 😤
Celebratory 🎉

→ App suggests meals that match emotional states.

📍 Location-Aware Discovery
Detects nearby restaurants
Prioritizes closest vendors
Reduces delivery friction
💰 Budget Filtering
Users input available money
App suggests realistic options
Eliminates “cart disappointment”
⚡ Fastest Delivery First
Highlights meals with shortest prep + delivery time
Ideal for urgent hunger decisions
⭐ Top Rated Meals
Surfaces highly rated dishes
Builds trust through social proof

🧠 Architecture

The app follows MVVM:

Model → Food, Restaurant, User Mood
View → SwiftUI screens
ViewModel → Business logic (filters, recommendations)

This ensures:
Scalability
Clean separation of concerns
Reusability across screens
